Postgraduate Research Programmes

Postgraduate Research (PGR) Programmes

At ABS, we create academic leaders and inculcate the mind of perpetual inquiry and rational questioning. Our desire to contribute to the world of learning lead us to promoting researchcurricula at all levels and with all sense of innovation and development.

Our postgraduate taught courses are challenging and intensive: we expect a lot from our students as much as they place high expectation on us. Our approach to accommodating your learning needs emphasises your ability to work independently, while supported by a world-class academic community trained to do so. Each of our students belong to departments or colleges, which are active academic communities working to deepen their knowledge in a particular field. Your department serves you through teaching and supervision, as well as extensive research resources to support your studies. Our departments often collaborate on joint courses and interdisciplinary research projects

ABS offers the following research courses;

 Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Accounting    Master of Philosophy (MPhil) in Accounting  Master of Sceince (MSc) in Accounting
Students are systematically encouraged to proceed to further levels by instituting an efficient examination and result processing system that shortens time lag between completing examinations and approving results by 45%.
