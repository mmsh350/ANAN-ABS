Undergraduate Professional (UGP) Programmes

ABS Diplomas serve a multitude of purposes for different students: they are sometimes used to bridge the gap between different phases of academic study, or to provide additional professional training and qualifications to participants seeking to remain relevant in the ever changing and competitive work environment. There are diplomas in a wide variety of subjects as listed below;

Code Course Name Duration
DMP Diploma in Management Practice 4 Semesters
DIF Diploma in Islamic Finance & Investments 4 Semesters
DTE Diploma in Technology Entrepreenurship 4 Semesters
DSE Diploma in Social Entrepreenurship 4 Semesters
DAI Diploma in Accounting Information Systems 4 Semesters
DSI Diploma in Supply Chain & International Trade 4 Semesters
