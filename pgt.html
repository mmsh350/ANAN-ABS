Postgraduate Taught (PGT) Programs

Learning experience at ABS is both fulfilling and dramatic in all the courses we offer. As our curriculum has gone through rigorous reviews by professors and indutry experts in order to breed the best candidates for the academia and the industry, we continue to make the learning environment as accomodative as we can.

Postgraduate taught causes at ABS are filled with exciting experience opportunities ranging from field learning periods, working group activities, short-period internships, explorative lecture series, meetings with industry and government leaders, engagements with professional organisations, and supporting students to build prototype ideas, products, and entities.
