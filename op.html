Open Professional Programmes

Open Professional (OP) Programmes

To be open to change, innovation and big thinking, one has to update his knowledge set with creative short courses from time to time. ABS is open to new experiences and diverse perspectives that will offer its trainees the opportunity to see the world beyond common sense. We offer a rich and innovative portfolio of in-person programmes, and a selection of online courses in a range of innovative formats, bringing the true ABS experience to our participants both in Africa and around the world. With an unrivalled ability to deliver learning globally, we equip individuals, teams and organisations with the skills they need to thrive and break barriers in the industry.

Whatever your goals, our wide range of short courses equip you with the mindset, capabilities and network for your future. Together we design and deliver learning solutions uniquely tailored to your organisation, your context, and your challenges so that we participate in creating a new intellectual you.
