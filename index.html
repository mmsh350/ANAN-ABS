The speed at which learning happens in the 21st Century coupled with the very influence of technology in the life itself motivates our drive to establish the uniquely designed ANAN University Business School (ABS). At ABS, we built a resilient and learner centered environment for serious learning and life.

The School is coded with the philosophy of redefining learning to enable the process of discovering students' unique talents take course. This signifies our commitment to treating all our students and partners with individual care and precision in identifying their needs.

Our learning environment is an advanced one guided by innovative and perpetually updated curriculum to achieve the goal of successful delivery of the needed skill-sets and talents. The peculiar approach we adopt in reaching and satisfying those we serve makes us a one-of-a-kind institution to reckon with.

We offer programmes that will chart your course to success in your existing or new career. Our taught and research postgraduate programmes in accounting and business administration are showcased with peculiar specialisations that makes you more competitive and relevant in the industry. We add to this a cache of Diploma programmes and short courses delivered to you physically and remotely to give you the best experience.

I therefore welcome you to ABS, our African version of the best academic and executive higher education opportunity.
